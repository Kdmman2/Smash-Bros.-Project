Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is the fifth installment in the Super Smash Bros. Series. Smash Ultimate was release on the Nintendo Switch on December 7th, 2018. One big selling point of this game was that it would feature every character ever introduced into all the other Super Smash Bros. games, as well as adding some more fighters into the mix. At launch, the game featured 74 playable characters. The game currently has 79 fighters, with 1 more confirmed fighter on the way. Nintendo also announced that there will be even more fighters being added to the game later on. Smash Ultimate was very positively received by fans. Some of the praise it received came from the game having a large cast of fighter, stages, soundtracks, as well as improving on the core gameplay from Smash 4 on the Wii U. In November of 2018, it was revealed that, at the time, Smash Ultimate was the most preordered game on the Switch, and the most preordered game in the Super Smash Bros. series.
